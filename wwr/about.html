📜 History

Founded in 1998 by river enthusiast Margo Rivers and veteran guide Sam “Cascade” Peterson, Whitewater Rush Expeditions began as a small outpost with two rafts and a dream. Over two decades, we navigated wild currents from the Gauley River to the Colorado rapids, growing into a trusted leader in adventure tourism. Our legacy is built on respect for waterways, community spirit, and training hundreds of guides who share the same fire. Today, we’ve hosted over 50,000 thrill-seekers, blending sustainable tourism with heart-pounding descents. From humble gear sheds to state-of-the-art eco-friendly basecamps, our history flows like the rivers we cherish — wild, resilient, and always forward.
